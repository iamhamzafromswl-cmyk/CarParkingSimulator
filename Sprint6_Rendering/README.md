# Sprint 6 - Rendering

## Task Description

This sprint focused on configuring the final visual output of the game. This includes dynamic/static lighting, shadow settings, ambient occlusion, post-processing effects, camera settings, and overall quality output to produce polished final visuals.

## Deliverables

*   **Screenshots/Video Output**: High-quality visual captures demonstrating the final rendering setup.
*   **Final Build**: The compiled game executable (committed to the repository).

## Detailed Elaboration (from Assignment Guide)

> Configure the final visual output of the game. Includes dynamic/static lighting, shadow settings, ambient occlusion, post-processing effects (bloom, color grading, depth of field), anti-aliasing, and render pipeline settings (URP/HDRP in Unity or Eevee/Cycles in Blender) to produce polished final visuals.
